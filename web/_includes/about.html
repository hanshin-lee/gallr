About gallr

gallr is an exhibitions discovery app for people who care about art and culture. We surface what's showing right now — and what's opening soon — so you can stay connected to the gallery scene in your city without having to hunt for it.

The app started from a simple frustration: great exhibitions come and go, and most people find out too late. gallr fixes that. From major institutions to independent spaces, from solo shows to group exhibitions, we bring the full picture together in one place — curated, searchable, and always up to date.
