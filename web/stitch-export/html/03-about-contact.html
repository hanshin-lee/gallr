03 / The Note

Bridging the Seoul Scene

Seoul’s art scene is vibrant, frantic, and often hidden behind heavy doors in Samcheong-dong and Hannam-dong. gallr was born from a frustration with the friction of finding these spaces.

We wanted to build something that felt like the galleries themselves—quiet, authoritative, and deeply focused on the work. Our mission is to democratize the 'insider' knowledge of the art world without diluting the prestige of the experience.

We believe that when art is discovered through a lens of respect and high-design, the connection between the viewer and the artist is amplified. This is our commitment to the craft.
