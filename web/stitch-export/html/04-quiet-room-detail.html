SEOUL / SPACE 77 | April 10 — May 15

The Quiet Room

Curatorial Note

A meditative exploration of negative space and the architecture of silence. Space 77 presents a curated selection of minimalist sculptures that challenge our perception of stillness.

The exhibition invites visitors to experience the "void" as a physical presence. Each piece serves as an anchor in the vast openness of the gallery, demanding a slower pace and heightened sensory awareness.
